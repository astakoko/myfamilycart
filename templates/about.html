About Create and share shopping lists

MyFamilyCart came to life as we (our) family needed a common place to save our shopping lists. The App lets you create different shopping carts (lists) where you can add and remove items.

The app also has a Topp50 page where you can see what kind of items/groceries you buy the most. Further statistics might be introduced down the road should the need arise. If you have ideas feel free to look under the Open Source section and add a github issue, or simply send a mail to myfamily@unialt.no

Privacy

Your email is mainly used for password reset requests and will not be shared with others! You will only receive emails if there is a security breach or other important issues like major changes affecting your user experience or database changes.

We collect yor words (items) for your predictions (autocomplete). These words are user specific and is not viewable / accessible between users. You are in full control of the word database and can delete them when ever you want in the Top50 view of the App.

We do not analyse or process your word (items) They are there for your convenience.

Open Source

This site / app is completely free and opensource! The code is hosted on GitHub where you can add issues (bugs / feature requests / feedback) contributing to the site.
